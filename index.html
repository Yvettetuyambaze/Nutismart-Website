About NutriSmart

Our Mission

To empower Rwandans with personalized, culturally-relevant nutrition guidance, leveraging AI technology to prevent non-communicable diseases and promote healthier lifestyles.

Our Vision

To become the leading AI-powered nutrition platform in Africa, significantly reducing the prevalence of diet-related NCDs and improving overall health outcomes across the continent.

44%

of deaths in Rwanda are due to NCDs

Our Journey

  • 2023 - Foundation
    NutriSmart was founded with the vision of leveraging AI to address Rwanda's nutrition challenges.
  • 2024 - Prototype Development
    Successfully developed and tested the first prototype of the NutriSmart app.
  • 2025 - Launch and Expansion
    NutriSmart will be Officially launched in Rwanda, with plans for expansion to other East African countries.

Frequently Asked Questions

How accurate is NutriSmart's food recognition?

Our AI model has been trained on over 900 Rwandan dishes and achieves an accuracy rate of 95% in identifying local foods.

Is my health data secure with NutriSmart?

Yes, we use industry-standard encryption and follow strict data protection protocols to ensure the privacy and security of all user information.

Can I use NutriSmart if I have specific dietary restrictions?

Absolutely! NutriSmart takes into account various dietary needs, including vegetarian, vegan, gluten-free, and specific health conditions when providing recommendations.
